The Mirror (Chapter One)

Peak into my book

I sit in this room…

I sit in this room and I wonder Hands on head as I ponder(hands on head) Do I remain limitless or my mind is just restless? If I did all the work, and didn’t over think maybe I wouldn’t collect less. Now, I become a prisoner to my own guilt. Judged and sentenced to suffer but only started in the mind. Now the manifestation of my Suffering has only tapped into the real world.(screams) Forgive me, forgive you.(kneel).
